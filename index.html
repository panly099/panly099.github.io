Hi, I am a postdoc researcher in OSRAM in Germany. My current research interests inlcude pedestrian detection, tracking and indoor scene modelling. I was a PhD candidate in Computer Vision Group in School of EECS in QMUL. I was co-supervised by Prof. Shaogang Gong and Dr. Yi-Zhe Song. My research interest was on cross-domain image matching.
